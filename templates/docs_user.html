Welcome to the {{ APP_NAME }} User Guide. This manual will help you understand how to extract entities from text and compare our statistical and neural models.

01 Getting Started

To begin your analysis, navigate to the Try Tool Page. You have two ways to input data:

  • Manual Input Paste any paragraph of text into the primary analysis vector box.
  • Presets Use one of our four presets (News, Tech, Finance, Business) to see the tool in action instantly.

02 Choosing Your Engine

CRF Model (Green)

Our Statistical model. It uses mathematical sequences to identify patterns quickly. Best for structured documents and predictable entities.

SpaCy Model (Purple)

Our Neural Network model. It understands context and semantic nuances. Best for complex, conversational, or diverse text inputs.

Comparison Mode

The most powerful way to use {{ APP_NAME }}. It runs both models simultaneously and provides a side-by-side benchmark of extraction quality and speed.

04 Common Questions

What is the "Quality Lead"?

The quality lead is awarded to the model that identifies more complex, multi-word entities (like full names or specific location addresses) with higher structural accuracy.

Why is SpaCy sometimes slower?

SpaCy uses heavy neural network calculations which require more processing power compared to the purely statistical (mathematical) nature of the CRF model.
