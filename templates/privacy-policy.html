Privacy Policy

Your data our priority

Research Data Processing

As an academic project, {{ APP_NAME }} is designed with high privacy standards. Any text you paste into the analysis vector is processed **entirely in-memory**. We do not store, log, or maintain records of your processed text on our servers. Once the extraction is complete, the data is flushed from our buffers.

No Personal Data Collection

We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information (PII) through our analysis tool.

Academic Integrity

This policy is written to comply with the academic guidelines of ILM College Sargodha. For technical details on the implementation, please refer to our Project Repository.
