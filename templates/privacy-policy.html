Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 26 March 2026

This Privacy Policy outlines how the Comparative NER Tool handles and protects user information during its operational cycle. As an academic research project, we prioritize data integrity and transparency.

1. Data Sovereignty

Your input text is processed in-memory for real-time Named Entity Recognition. We do not persist, store, or warehouse any content analyzed through the platform once the transmission cycle is complete.

2. Performance Metrics

Our servers log anonymous performance data—specifically processing time and entity counts—to benchmark the evolution from CRF to Neural architectures. No personally identifiable information is associated with these logs.

3. Academic Scope

This platform serves as a Semester 3 project for the BSCS Batch 24-28 at ILM College Sargodha. Data handling protocols are strictly aligned with academic research ethics.

4. Security

We implement hardware-level encryption (SSL/TLS) for data in transit during common processing tasks to ensure that your text inputs remain confidential during analysis.
