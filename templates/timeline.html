Development Cycle

The 8-Week Sprint

From 26 Jan 2026 to 26 Mar 2026 PROJECT COMPLETED

Weeks 1-2: Finding Our Way

Team Learning Phase

The first two weeks were all about exploration and learning. After Sir Abdur-Rehman gave us the green light on our NER proposal, we spent most of our time getting familiar with the technologies. While Hassan led the architectural planning, Mudassir and Saad worked hard to master Python OOP, the Git workflow, and the principles of Tkinter design.

Weeks 3-6: Building the Core

This was the period where most of the heavy lifting happened. We trained our custom CRF model using the CoNLL2003 dataset, achieving a solid 90.32% F1 score. During this same time, we completely built out a functional desktop application using Tkinter, ensuring that all our logic worked perfectly in a local environment.

Weeks 7-8: The Web & AWS Pivot

With a working desktop app in hand, we decided to take things a step further. In the final two weeks, we pivoted to a web-based framework using Flask. This allowed us to build a more professional portal, prepare comprehensive documentation, and finally deploy the entire system on an AWS-EC2 server at ner.orbin.dev.

Final Submission

By the end of March, we had a fully operational platform at ner.orbin.dev. We finalized our User and Developer guides and submitted the project for our Artificial Intelligence course under Sir Abdur-Rehman.
